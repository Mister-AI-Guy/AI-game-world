How it works
GA
Genetic Algorithm

Each generation the best agents survive and reproduce. Mutation and crossover create the next generation automatically.

NN
Neural Networks

Every agent runs a small feedforward neural network. No gradient descent — just evolution selecting for performance.

100%
Runs in Your Browser

No server, no install. Pure vanilla JavaScript. Training runs live while you watch — all computation happens client-side.
