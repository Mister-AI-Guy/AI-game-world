🧠 How it works

🧬
Genetic Algorithm

Each generation, the best-performing agents survive and reproduce. Mutation and crossover create the next generation.

🕸
Neural Networks

Each AI agent has a small neural network as its brain. No gradient descent — just evolution selecting for fitness.

👁
Watch in Real Time

All training runs in your browser. No server, no waiting — pure JavaScript. Watch agents improve generation by generation.

➕
Train Your Own

Spawn your own AI agent, adjust population size and mutation rate, and watch it learn independently.
